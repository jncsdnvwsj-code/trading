TECHNICAL DETAILS
──────────────────

Optimization Algorithm: SLSQP (Sequential Least Squares Programming)

Black-Litterman Formula:
μ_BL = μ_prior + Σ P^T (P Σ P^T + Ω)^(-1) (Q - P μ_prior)

Transaction Cost Model:
Cost = |Δw| × Portfolio_Value × (transaction_cost + slippage) / 2

Data Source: Yahoo Finance via yfinance

Default Assets: AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, NVDA, JPM, XOM, JNJ

Historical Period: 5 years (customizable)
